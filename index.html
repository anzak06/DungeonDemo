Controls:

  • WASD to move
  • Arrow keys pan camera
  • 't' to take items
  • Click an item from the inventory to equip it
  • Hover over a square with the mouse and press 'l' to inspect it"
  • Press 'z' or click the first skill on the action bar to Melee.
  • Press 'x' or click the second skill on the action bar to Shoot (10 Energy).
